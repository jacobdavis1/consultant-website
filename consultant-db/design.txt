Scaffolding Command, DB First:
Scaffold-DbContext "Host=<database site, eg drona.elephantsql.com>;Database=<database name>;Username=<username>;Password=<password>" Npgsql.EntityFrameworkCore.PostgreSQL -OutputDir Database

High-level Table Structures:

 Case
 - caseId (primary key)
 - caseTitle

 CaseNotes
 - noteId (primary key)
 - caseId (foreign key)
 - content

 Appointment
 - appointmentId (primary key)
 - caseId (foreign key)
 - appointmentDateTime
 - appointmentTitle

 Client
 - clientId (primary key)
 - firstName
 - middleName
 - lastName
 - email

 CaseClient
 - caseId (foreign key)
 - clientId (foreign key)
